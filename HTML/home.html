About Us

At E-Waste Finder, we're committed to addressing the pressing problem of electronic waste disposal. Millions of tons of discarded electronic devices threaten the environment and human health annually. We provide a convenient solution by collecting e-waste from individuals and businesses, ensuring proper recycling. Our dedicated team transports the products to authorized recyclers in India, following best practices. Join us in creating a cleaner, more sustainable future by contributing to resource conservation and reducing environmental pollution. Together, we can make a difference.

What We Offer

  • Pickup of electronic waste from your location
  • Environmentally responsible disposal of e-waste
  • Collaboration with authorized recyclers in India
  • Transparent and ethical recycling process
  • Contribution to a cleaner and safer environment
